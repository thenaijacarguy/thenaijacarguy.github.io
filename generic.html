About Me

I'M A SENIOR DATA ANALYST, WITH OVER 5 YEARS OF EXPERIENCE IN THE AUTOMOBILE, RETAIL, TECH AND EDUCATION INDUSTRIES. I POSSESS EXTENSIVE EXPERIENCE IN BUSINESS AND PEOPLE ANALYTICS WITH PROFICIENCY IN PYTHON, R, TABLEAU, POWER BI, AND ALL DIALECTS OF SQL. WHEN I'M NOT GENERATING INSIGHTS FROM BIG DATA, I'M TUTORING AND MENTORING A GROUP OF PROFESSIONAL DATA ANALYSTS.

I HAD MY FIRST DEGREE IN AUTOMOBILE TECHNOLOGY AND THEN PROCEEDED TO WORKING WITH THE LEADING AUTOMOBILE RETAIL FIRM IN AFRICA FOR A COUPLE OF YEARS BEFORE MY DRIVE FOR BEHIND-THE-SCENE ANSWERS LED ME TO DATA, SO I TOOK A LEAP OF FAITH AND PIVOTED INTO TECH. WORKING WITH DATA HAS BROADENED MY HORIZON AND SATISFIED MY CURIOSITY.

DATA ANALYTICS TO ME IS LIKING WORKING AT THE BACK-STAGE OF A BIG MUSICAL OR OPENING UP A NEW TOY TO LEARN WHAT MAKES IT TICK. I LOVE WORKING BEHIND THE SCENES OF BIG BUSINESS DECISIONS KNOWING I'M A HUGE INFLUENCE IN THE DECISION MAKING PROCESS WHICH IS NOT JUST BASED ON OPINIONS BUT ENTIRELY ON METRICS AND INSIGHTS.

OUTSIDE OF ANALYZING DATA, I LOVE TO VISIT THE BEACH AND GO SEE SOME ART. I LOVE TUTORING AS THIS CHALLENGES ME TO BE AT MY BEST, AND ALSO GIVES ME THE OPPORTUNITY TO PAY FORWARD THE INVESTMENT THAT WAS MADE ON ME.I LOVE THE PIANO AND ANY GENRE OF MUSIC THAT INCORPORATES HEAVY GUITAR RIFFS.
